--- layout: post title: "Public Holidays in the World in 2022" ---

On which days do humans celebrate their public holidays? I was wondering if nations tended to cluster along certain dates and time periods when it comes to public holidays. Below is a calendar heatmap of the public holidays of the UN member states in 2022.

The 3 most celebrated public holidays, based on the gregorian calendar, are:

  • New Year's Day (Jan 1st): celebrated in 156 countries.
  • Christmas (Dec 25th): celebrated in 121 countries.
  • Labor Day (May 1st): celebrated in 121 countries.
